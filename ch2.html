Stress and strain measures

To quantitatively describe the mechanical behaviour of organic soils, it is essential to establish a consistent set of measures for stress and strain. While soil is a complex material—and organic soil, even more so—it is treated as a continuum to facilitate theoretical modelling. This section outlines the definitions adopted for stress and strain throughout this study. Emphasis is also placed on the choice of appropriate measures for large deformation.
